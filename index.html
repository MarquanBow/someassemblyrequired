About Me

Built Layer by Layer

At Some Assembly Required, we bring your 3D printing ideas to life—no matter what they are. While we do not create original models, we specialize in printing almost anything as long as you can provide the file. We do light modifications to ensure the best results, but our focus is on delivering exactly what you want

Started as a way to fund my own 3D printer (half-jokingly), this small business is all about sharing the power of 3D printing with those who do not own a printer themselves. Whether you are a gamer, cosplayer, hobbyist, or just someone looking for practical items to make life easier, I am here to help

What sets Some Assembly Required apart? Reasonable pricing, a personal touch, and a turnaround time of no more than three weeks. Plus, if a misprint happens, reprints are on me—and they’re my top priority.

Think of me as the underdog taking on the big guys by doing the stuff they won’t. I’m here to make your 3D printing experience easy, affordable, and fun.
